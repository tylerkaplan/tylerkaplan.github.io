penguins

Fun Facts:

  • Penguins in Antarctica have no land based predators.
  • Emperor Penguins can stay underwater for around 20 minutes at a time.
  • While other birds have wings for flying, penguins have adapted flippers to help them swim in the water.

Mount Fuji

Fun Facts

  • Mt Fuji has three volcanoes in one
  • Mt Fuji was first climbed by a monk
  • Mt Fuji is an active volcano and last erupted in 1707
  • Mt Fuji is surrounded by five lakes
  • Mt Fuji is the most climbed mountain in the world
